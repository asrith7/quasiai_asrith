2. Introduction

Single crystal growth is a critical process in materials science and engineering, underpinning the production of high-performance materials used in semiconductors, optics, and advanced alloys...

3. Objectives

  • Develop a PINN framework tailored to simulate single crystal growth processes.

  • Compare PINN performance with conventional simulation techniques.
  • Identify key process parameters for optimizing crystal growth.

Single crystal growth processes, such as Czochralski and Bridgman methods, are governed by complex interactions...

This project involves integrating physics-informed neural networks to enhance the accuracy of single crystal growth simulations...
